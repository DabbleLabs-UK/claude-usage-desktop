When your login is about to expire, the app makes a tiny check-in to Claude (a handful of tokens, a few times a day) to refresh it — so your usage keeps showing without re-signing-in. This adds a very small amount to your own usage. Turn off to refresh manually instead (open Claude Code yourself when the numbers pause).
